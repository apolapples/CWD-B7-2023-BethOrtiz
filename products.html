Did you know?

Lemonade has a long history and is believed to have originated in ancient Egypt around 1,500 BC. The Egyptians sweetened lemon juice with honey to create a refreshing beverage. The popularity of lemons grew during the 18th century when Napoleon Bonaparte, the French military and political leader, realized that lemon juice could be used to prevent scurvy among his troops. Christopher Columbus is credited with introducing lemons to the New World during his voyages in the late 15th century. During the Renaissance, lemons were highly prized and were displayed as a symbol of wealth and prestige in paintings. Menton, a town in the French Riviera, holds an annual Lemon Festival ("Fête du Citron") where enormous sculptures made of lemons and oranges are displayed.

Fresh Harvested Lemons

Freshly harvested lemons are a sensory delight, boasting vibrant hues of yellow and an invigorating citrus fragrance that fills the air. Each plump and juicy fruit holds the promise of a tangy burst of flavor, ready to enliven dishes, beverages, and desserts with their zesty essence.

Exclusive Lemon Recipes

Lemons recipes offer a delightful balance of sweet and tangy flavors, dancing harmoniously on the taste buds. Whether it's a refreshing glass of homemade lemonade or a zesty lemon-infused pasta, these recipes add a burst of sunshine to any dining experience.

Variety of Our Exclusive Refreshing Lemon drinks

Enjoy our variety of exclusive Lemon Recipes





Lemon Juice

Lemon in a glass, a classic thirst quencher, requires only three simple ingredients: freshly squeezed lemon juice, water, and a touch of sweetener (such as sugar or honey) to balance the tanginess. The result is a refreshing beverage that captures the essence of lemons in every sip.



Lemon-Macarons

Lemon macarons are a delightful treat crafted with just a handful of ingredients: almond flour, powdered sugar, egg whites, and a generous zesting of fresh lemons for a burst of citrus flavor. These light and airy confections are then filled with a luscious lemon-infused buttercream, creating a perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess in every bite.



Lemon Beer

Lemon beer, a refreshing and citrusy delight, typically combines the classic ingredients of beer with the zesty essence of lemons. It consists of malted barley, hops, water, and yeast, along with the addition of lemon zest or lemon juice during the brewing process, lending it a bright and tangy flavor profile.



Lemon Cookies

Lemon cookies are a delectable treat made with just a few simple ingredients: butter, sugar, eggs, flour, and the refreshing zest of fresh lemons. The lemon zest infuses the cookies with a delightful citrus aroma and tangy flavor, making them a delightful accompaniment to any occasion.



Salmon in Lemon

Salmon in lemon is a succulent and flavorful dish that requires only a handful of ingredients: fresh salmon fillets, lemon juice, olive oil, and a pinch of salt and pepper for seasoning. The tangy lemon marinade complements the rich and buttery taste of the salmon, creating a delightful seafood delight that's both simple and satisfying.



Lemon Popsicles

Refreshing lemon popsicles are a delightful summer treat made with just three ingredients: freshly squeezed lemon juice, water, and a touch of sweetener, such as honey or sugar, to balance the tartness. Once frozen, these zesty popsicles offer a burst of citrusy goodness, perfect for beating the heat and enjoying a taste of sunshine on a stick.



Pasta in Lemon

Pasta in lemon is a delightful and light dish that requires only a handful of ingredients: cooked pasta of your choice, freshly squeezed lemon juice, olive oil, grated Parmesan cheese, and a sprinkle of salt and black pepper. The lemon-infused sauce adds a tangy brightness to the pasta, making it a simple yet delicious option for a quick and satisfying meal.



Lemon Ice Cream

Lemon ice cream is a delightful frozen dessert made with just a few key ingredients: heavy cream, sweetened condensed milk, freshly squeezed lemon juice, and lemon zest. The combination of creamy richness and zesty lemon flavor creates a luscious and refreshing treat that's perfect for satisfying any sweet cravings.
